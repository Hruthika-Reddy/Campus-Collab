About the Hackathon

The Hackathon Event 2024 is a premier competition where participants create innovative solutions to real-world problems using cutting-edge technologies. This event brings together the brightest minds to test their skills in coding, design, and problem-solving.

In our fast-paced digital world, this hackathon provides a platform to develop practical skills through hands-on projects guided by industry leaders and tech experts.

Competition Rules

To ensure fair competition and maintain integrity, all participants must follow these guidelines:

Code Usage

Only original code may be used. External libraries require approval and proper attribution.

Team Composition

Teams of 2-4 members from the same institution. Cross-institution teams require special permission.

Tools & Technologies

Open-source tools are encouraged. Commercial software requires prior approval.

Submission Timing

Projects must be submitted before deadlines. Late submissions will be penalized.

Evaluation Criteria

Projects judged on innovation, functionality, design, and potential impact.

Original Work

All submissions must be original work created during the hackathon period.
