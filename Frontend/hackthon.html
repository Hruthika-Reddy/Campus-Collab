About the Hackathon

TechHack 2024 is an intercollegiate hackathon bringing together the brightest minds from top engineering colleges across India. This 48-hour coding marathon challenges participants to solve real-world problems through innovative technology solutions.

With themes ranging from AI & Machine Learning to Sustainable Tech and Healthcare Innovations, TechHack provides a platform for students to showcase their skills, learn from industry experts, and network with peers.

Themes & Tracks

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning: Build intelligent systems that can learn and adapt
  • Blockchain & Web3: Develop decentralized applications for the future internet
  • Healthcare Technology: Create solutions to improve medical care and accessibility
  • Sustainable Development: Innovate for a greener planet with climate tech solutions
  • FinTech Innovations: Revolutionize financial services with technology
  • Open Innovation: Any groundbreaking idea that doesn't fit the above categories

Rules & Guidelines

  • Teams of 2-4 members from the same or different colleges
  • All code must be written during the hackathon (no pre-written code)
  • Use of open-source libraries and APIs is allowed
  • Projects must be submitted by the deadline to be eligible for prizes
  • All participants must adhere to the code of conduct
  • Judges' decisions are final

Code of Conduct: We expect all participants to maintain a respectful and inclusive environment. Harassment of any kind will not be tolerated.
