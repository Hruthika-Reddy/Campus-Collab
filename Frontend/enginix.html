About the Summit

Enginix Tech Summit is the premier gathering for engineering professionals, researchers, and students to explore cutting-edge technologies and future trends in engineering. This three-day conference features keynote presentations, technical workshops, and networking opportunities with industry leaders.

With themes ranging from Artificial Intelligence in Engineering to Sustainable Technologies and Advanced Manufacturing, Enginix provides a platform for knowledge sharing and professional development.
