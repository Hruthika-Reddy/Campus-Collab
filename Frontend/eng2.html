Unit I: Professional Presentation (6 Contact Hours)

Preparing Your Presentation

  • Researching and collecting materials
  • Identifying key points and structure

Developing PowerPoint Slides

Best practices for creating professional presentation slides:

PPT Design Checklist

  • One main idea per slide
  • Limited text (6x6 rule: 6 lines, 6 words)
  • High contrast colors for readability
  • Consistent font styles and sizes
  • Relevant visuals/graphics
  • Proper citations for references
  • Slide numbers and footer

Delivery Techniques

Practice Scenario: Product Launch Presentation

  • Voice modulation and pace

Professional Emails

Subject: Meeting Request - Project Update
To: manager@company.com
Cc: team@company.com
Dear Mr. Sharma, I hope this email finds you well. I would like to request a meeting to discuss the current status of the Phoenix Project and address some challenges we've encountered. Proposed Meeting Details: - Date: June 15, 2023 - Time: 2:00 PM - 3:00 PM - Location: Conference Room B Please let me know if this time works for you or suggest an alternative. I've attached the preliminary report for your review. Looking forward to your confirmation. Best regards, [Your Full Name] [Your Position] [Your Contact Information]

Writing Minutes

Unit IV: People Skills (6 Contact Hours)

Professional Conversations

Techniques for effective communication with different stakeholders:

2-Minute Small Talk Scenarios

Talking to Executives

"Good morning, Mr. Johnson. I've been following the company's expansion plans with great interest. How do you see this affecting our department's priorities in the coming quarter?"

Talking to Colleagues

"Hi Priya, I noticed you handled that client complaint really well yesterday. What approach worked best for you in that situation?"

Talking to Your Boss

"Ms. Kapoor, I wanted to get your perspective on the new project timeline. Do you have 2 minutes to share your thoughts on how we might streamline the process?"

Business Dining Etiquette

Dining Etiquette Checklist

  • RSVP promptly to invitations
  • Wait to be seated or follow host's lead
  • Napkin on lap upon sitting
  • Start eating only after host begins
  • Use utensils from the outside in
  • Break bread rather than cutting it
  • Avoid discussing business until host initiates
  • Thank host before leaving
  • Send thank-you note within 24 hours
