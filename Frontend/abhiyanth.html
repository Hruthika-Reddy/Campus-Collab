About Abhiyanth

Abhiyanth is our college's annual 3-day cultural and technical extravaganza that brings together students from all departments for a celebration of talent, innovation, and fun. This year's edition promises to be bigger and better with exciting pre-events, star performances, and delicious food stalls.

From technical competitions to cultural performances, and from marathon runs to celebrity shows, Abhiyanth has something for everyone!
