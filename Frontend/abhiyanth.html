TechFusion 2024 is a premier technical symposium that brings together the brightest minds in technology and innovation. This three-day event features cutting-edge competitions, workshops, and networking opportunities with industry leaders.

Competition Rules

To ensure fair competition and maintain integrity, all participants must follow these guidelines:

Original Work

All submissions must be original work created during the event. Pre-existing projects are not allowed.

Team Composition

Teams must consist of 2-4 members from the same institution. Cross-institution teams require special permission.

Time Management

Projects must be submitted before the deadline. Late submissions will be penalized or disqualified.

Innovation Focus

Solutions should demonstrate innovation and creativity in addressing the given problem statements.

Tools & Resources

Only approved tools and resources may be used. Internet access will be provided but monitored.

Judging Criteria

Projects will be judged on functionality, innovation, technical complexity, and presentation quality.
