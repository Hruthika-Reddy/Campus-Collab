Aadhya - CSE Event 2024

Event Description

Aadhya is the flagship event of the Computer Science Department, celebrating innovation and technical excellence. This event brings together CSE students from across the region to compete, learn, and showcase their skills.

Event Details

Date: March 10-12, 2025

Time: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM daily

Location: Computer Science Department, Main Campus

Eligibility: Open to all CSE students

Featured Events

Code for Apple

A competitive coding challenge where participants solve algorithmic problems to win prizes.

Tech Quiz

Test your knowledge of computer science fundamentals and current tech trends.

Project Expo

Showcase your innovative projects to judges and fellow students.

Hackathon

24-hour coding marathon to build solutions for real-world problems.

Rules

  • Participants must be currently enrolled CSE students
  • Teams of 2-4 members for group events
  • All code must be original work
  • Judges' decisions will be final
