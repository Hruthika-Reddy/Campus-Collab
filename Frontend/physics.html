Course Description

This course covers fundamental physics concepts with emphasis on engineering applications, including mechanics, thermodynamics, electromagnetism, and modern physics.

Course Content

Unit 1: Classical Mechanics

  • Newton's Laws of Motion
  • Work, Energy, and Power
  • Rotational Dynamics
  • Gravitation and Orbital Mechanics

Unit 2: Thermodynamics

  • Laws of Thermodynamics
  • Heat Engines and Refrigerators
  • Kinetic Theory of Gases

Unit 3: Electromagnetism

  • Electrostatics and Magnetostatics
  • Maxwell's Equations
  • AC/DC Circuits
