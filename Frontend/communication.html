23EG1181 - English Language Communication Skills Lab-I

Course Overview

This lab course enhances English communication skills through multimedia tools, focusing on pronunciation, fluency, and practical usage in professional contexts like debates, presentations, and group discussions.

Course Details

Course Category L-T-P Credits
HSMC 1-0-3 2.5

Course Objectives

  1. Enable individualized language learning through computer-aided multimedia
  2. Teach English speech sounds, word accent, and intonation
  3. Provide practice for daily English usage
  4. Improve fluency and reduce mother tongue influence
  5. Train students in debates, group discussions, and public speaking

Tips for Success

  • Practice daily: Use language apps like Duolingo or HelloTalk
